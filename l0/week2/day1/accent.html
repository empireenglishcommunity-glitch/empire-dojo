Week 2 • Day 1 • Fricatives (/f/ /v/ /s/ /z/ /θ/ /ð/) + Vowels (/ɑː/ /oʊ/ /ʊ/ /uː/)

/f/ و /v/: الأسنان العلوية على الشفة السفلية. /f/ = هواء فقط. /v/ = نفس الوضع + اهتزاز.

🔊 Target Sounds: /f/, /v/

📝 Minimal Pairs

fan / van
fine / vine
ferry / very
feel / veal

🎯 Practice Words

five • very • love • live • give • have

🎙️ Say This

"I have five friends. Every Friday we visit my favorite place. I love it very much."
