Week 8 • Day 2 • Schwa Usage — The Reduced Vowel of Unstressed Syllables

النهايات زي -er و -or و -ar بدون نبر غالبًا بتختصر لصوت شوا + لمسة خفيفة من الـ r، مش حرف علة كامل وواضح.

🔊 Target Sounds: schwa /ə/ in the -er, -or, -ar endings

📝 Minimal Pairs

teacher (schwa ending, correct) / teach-OR (over-pronounced, foreign)
doctor (schwa ending, correct) / doc-TOR (over-pronounced, foreign)

🎯 Practice Words

teacher • doctor • sugar • water • better • actor • color • matter

🎙️ Say This

"My teacher is also a doctor — the water needs a little sugar, no matter the color, because the actor was better than his brother."
