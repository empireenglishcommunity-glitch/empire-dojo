Week 3 • Day 2 • Yod-Dropping — Losing the /j/ Glide After Alveolar Consonants

نفس الحذف بيحصل بعد /s/ و/l/ في كلمات زي suit وlure — بدون صوت /j/ قبل حرف العلة /uː/.

🔊 Target Sounds: yod-dropping after /s/, /l/

📝 Minimal Pairs

suit (American: soot) / suit (British-influenced: syoot)

🎯 Practice Words

suit • lure • resume • suitable • illuminate • salute

🎙️ Say This

"That suit is suitable for the interview — bring your resume, and the lure of a new job made him salute the offer."
