جمل الصلة غير التعريفية (بين فواصل) والجمل التابعة الطويلة عادة بتاخد وقفة قصيرة من الجانبين، بتفصلها كمعلومة إضافية.

🔊 Target Sounds: pausing before and after relative/subordinate clauses

📝 Minimal Pairs

My brother, | who lives in Canada, | is visiting. (correct pausing) / My brother who lives in Canada is visiting. (no pause, less clear for a long clause)

🎯 Practice Words

My brother, who lives in Canada, • Because it was raining, • The company, which was founded in 1990,

🎙️ Say This

"My brother, who lives in Canada, is visiting soon — because it was raining last time, we decided to stay home, but the company, which was founded in 1990, is doing well."
