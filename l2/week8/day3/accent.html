🔊 Target Sounds: pausing in lists and multi-part explanations

📝 Minimal Pairs

first, ... second, ... third, ... (paced, clear structure) / firstsecondthird (rushed, unclear structure)

🎯 Practice Words

first, • second, • and third, • there are three reasons:

🎙️ Say This

"There are three reasons for this: first, the cost; second, the time; and third, the risk — my plan has two parts, first research, then action."
