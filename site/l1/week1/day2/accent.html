Week 1 • Day 2 • Word Stress Foundations — Content Words vs. Function Words

الكلمات الوظيفية (a, the, to, of, for) نادرًا ما يكون عليها نبر في الكلام الطبيعي. صوتها بيصغر وحرف العلة فيها بيتحول لصوت الشوا /ə/.

🔊 Target Sounds: function word reduction: articles and prepositions

📝 Minimal Pairs

a (weak /ə/) / a (strong /eɪ/, only for emphasis)
to (weak /tə/) / two (strong /tuː/)
for (weak /fər/) / four (strong /fɔːr/)

🎯 Practice Words

a • the • to • of • for • and • at • in

🎙️ Say This

"I want to go to the store to buy a present for my friend."
