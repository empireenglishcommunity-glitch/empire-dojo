🎯 Accent Drill

Week 4 • Day 2 • Glottal Stop T — Before Syllabic N

مش كل /t/ قبل n بتاخد وقفة حنجرية — بس لما الـ n تشكل مقطعها لوحدها بدون حرف علة. قارن water (فيه حرف علة، فبيتحول لفلاب) مع button (الـ n لوحدها كمقطع، فبتاخد وقفة حنجرية).

🔊 Target Sounds: distinguishing glottal-stop words from flap-T words

📝 Minimal Pairs

water (flap) / button (glottal stop)
better (flap) / mountain (glottal stop)
matter (flap) / certain (glottal stop)

🎯 Practice Words

water • button • better • mountain • matter • certain • little • written

🎙️ Say This

"It doesn't matter — I'm certain the water is better near the mountain, and it's written on a little button by the trail."
