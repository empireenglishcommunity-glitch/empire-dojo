🎯 Accent Drill

Week 5 • Day 2 • Vowel-to-Vowel Linking — Inserting /w/ or /j/ Glides

لما الكلمة الأولى تنتهي بصوت متحرك أمامي ممدود (زي /iː/ في she أو /eɪ/ في day)، بيظهر صوت /j/ صغير (زي y في yes) يوصلها بالحرف المتحرك اللي بعده.

🔊 Target Sounds: /j/ glide inserted between spread vowels

📝 Minimal Pairs

she asked (glided) / she. Asked. (choppy)
I am (glided) / I. Am. (choppy)
day off (glided) / day. Off. (choppy)

🎯 Practice Words

she asked • I am • day off • we are • the office • my aunt

🎙️ Say This

"She asked if I am taking a day off, but we are going to the office with my aunt anyway."
